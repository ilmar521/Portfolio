Development of a loyalty system in a retail chain.
Previous Next
A loyalty points system has been developed in the customer's ERP system with the following functionalities:

● Accrual of bonus points to customers based on specific parameters, with different accrual rules for different customer categories.
● Redemption of points as discounts on purchases, within specific limits depending on the customer's status. The system intelligently distributes bonus points based on other product promotions, aiming to calculate the maximum discount for the customer's purchase.
● Automatic expiration of points after a certain period of non-usage. Each bonus points accrual has its own date and separate accounting (similar to FIFO accounting). Automatic bonus point accruals are triggered on a customer's birthday and other predetermined events.
● Mechanism for one-time bonus point allocations with notifications sent to customers. Due to the potential high volume, notifications are sent in batches through a buffer. Integration with WhatsApp allows duplicate notifications to be sent via API. Customer registration in the loyalty system is confirmed by the customer through WhatsApp or SMS using a special link that triggers a webhook in the ERP system.

The loyalty points system provides various features for rewarding and engaging customers, while integrating with WhatsApp for seamless communication and confirmation processes.
